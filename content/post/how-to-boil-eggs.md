Boiling eggs seems simple, but getting the exact yolk consistency you want requires simple timing.

## Ingredients & Tools

- 6 large eggs
- 4 cups of water
- Ice water bath

## Step-by-Step Instructions

1. **Boil Water**: Bring 4 cups of water to a rolling boil in a pot.
2. **Add Eggs**: Carefully lower the eggs into the boiling water using a slotted spoon.
3. **Timer Settings**:
   - Soft Boiled: **6 minutes**
   - Medium Boiled: **8 minutes**
   - Hard Boiled: **10 minutes**
4. **Cool Down**: Transfer eggs immediately into an ice water bath for 5 minutes before peeling.

{{< callout type="info" title="Easy Peeling Tip" >}}
Adding a pinch of baking soda or vinegar to the boiling water makes peeling eggshells much easier!
{{< /callout >}}
